https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~kriz/cifar.html

5 training batches, one test batch
  - each batch has 10000 images and labels
  - each image is a 3072x1 numpy vector
  - 3 color channels, 1024 points per channel (32x32 images)

Ten Classes
  0. airplane
  1. automobile
  2. bird
  3. cat
  4. deer
  5. dog
  6. frog
  7. horse
  8. ship
  9. truck

Both the MNIST and Fashion MNIST datasets contain 70000 images
  - 60000 training images and labels each
  - 10000 testing images and labels each

The Cifar-10 dataset contains 5 training batches (each with 10000 images and
labels) and one testing batch. The labels are as follows-
0: airplane
1: automobile
2: bird
3: cat
4: deer
5: dog
6: frog
7: horse
8: ship
9: truck

Each image is a 32x32 image with RGV values (32*32*3=3072 pixels per image per
batch).
